A world embedded in fascinating history

A long time ago, in a world where magic comes from the stars and is as common as fire, a group of talented individuals gathered at the top of the tallest mountain to study the heavenly bodies in order to better understand this power and how to control it.

Those were the first Spellgazers, who shared their knowledge with the world, bringing its civilizations to prosperity.

And then one day, a great fog engulfed the world, cutting its connection to the stars, resulting in the disappearance of magic and the Spellgazers who mastered it.

Many searched the lost sanctuary of the Spellgazers looking for the reason of their vanishing and to retrieve their magic, but all of them, without exception, were lost to the fog.

...until you arrived.
