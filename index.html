Long ago, atop the tallest mountain, a gathering of gifted individuals assembled. Their purpose: to scrutinize the celestial bodies, unraveling the mysteries of this power and mastering its control.

Those were the first Spellgazers, who shared their knowledge with the world, bringing its civilizations to prosperity.

And then one day, a great fog engulfed the world, cutting its connection to the stars, resulting in the disappearance of magic and the Spellgazers who mastered it.

Many searched the lost sanctuary of the Spellgazers looking for the reason of their disappear and to retrieve their magic, but all of them, without exception, were lost to the fog.
